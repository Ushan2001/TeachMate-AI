# TeachMate AI

**An Integrated AI-Powered Grading System & Teacher Guidance Automation for O/L ICT Education in Sri Lanka**

TeachMate AI is a comprehensive research project developed at the Faculty of Computing, Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (SLIIT). It leverages state-of-the-art AI, Computer Vision, and Graph Neural Networks to revolutionize the grading and learning experience for ICT students and teachers.

---

## 📈 Impact Results

- **84.9%** Grading Time Saved
- **0.83** F1-Score for AI Assessments
- **14.4%** Learning Improvement in Students

---

## 🚀 Key Research Components

### 1. VLM-Based OCR & AI Grading
- **Lead:** Pathiraja P.U.M.
- **Function:** Utilizes Vision-Language Models (VLM) for advanced OCR to digitize handwritten student answer sheets and perform automated, context-aware grading.

### 2. Knowledge Graphs & BKT/GNN
- **Lead:** Wanniarachchi W.A.P.M.
- **Function:** Employs Graph Neural Networks (GNN) and Bayesian Knowledge Tracing (BKT) to model student knowledge and provide automated reteaching guidance.

### 3. Bloom's Taxonomy Question Generation
- **Lead:** Hettiarachchi R.H.
- **Function:** Automated generation of question papers aligned with Bloom's Taxonomy levels to ensure balanced and effective assessments.

### 4. Personalized Learning Plans
- **Lead:** Jayasooriya L.T.
- **Function:** A mobile-first recommendation engine that generates tailored learning paths based on individual student performance gaps.
